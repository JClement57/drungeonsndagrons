The party then questioned Turok and discovered that the Reavers are headquartered at an abandoned lumber mill an hour east along the river. The party, along with their new friend Norin the dwarf, disguised themselves as Reavers and set out for the mill in the middle of the night. They arrived and infiltrated the mill and found where the prisoners were being kept before fighting broke out... Will they survive and free the captives or will they themselves become victims of the trafficking ring?
