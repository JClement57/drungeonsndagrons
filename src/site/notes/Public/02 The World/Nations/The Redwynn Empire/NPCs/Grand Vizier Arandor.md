## Overview
**Arándor Elarion** serves as Grand Vizier to [[Public/02 The World/Nations/The Redwynn Empire/NPCs/Emperor Seamus Redwynn II\|Emperor Seamus Redwynn II]] — the Empire's foremost advisor on matters of religion, magic, and celestial affairs. He holds the rare distinction of being blessed by **both [[Public/02 The World/History & Lore/Celestial Pantheon/Solarin\|Solarin]] and [[Public/02 The World/History & Lore/Celestial Pantheon/Lunira\|Lunira]]**, making him an anomaly in a world where clergy typically devote themselves to one celestial. He interprets this dual blessing not as divided loyalty, but as a singular calling: to embody **balance between light and mystery, order and change**.

### Appearance
A half-elf man with silver hair and mismatched eyes — one gold (Solarin), one silver (Lunira)

### Personality
Genuinely balanced — will argue the Emperor's opposition's case if no one else will. This is how he earned the respect of the emperor and a seat in his court.

## Responsibilities
- Celestial counsel — interprets omens, seams activity, and divine resonance on behalf of the crown
- Magical oversight — reviews arcane projects with imperial implications; not a battlefield caster but a diagnostic one
- Serves as the Emperor's formal liaison to [[Public/02 The World/Religion/The Order of the Celestium\|the Order of the Celestium]]
- Acts as **devil's advocate by appointment** — Seamus has formalized his role as the person who argues the other side before any major decision

## History
Arandor served as Emperor Redwynn's spiritual and educational tutor prior to his ascension to the throne. The emperor has always respected Arandor's perspective on the world and, upon his coronation, elevated Arandor to be his chief advisor.
