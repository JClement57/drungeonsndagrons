# Overworld
### Humans
***Origin/Ancestry:*** Created by Solarin during the creation of Caelthys  
***Where they typically live:*** Humans are spread all over realm of Caelthys  
***Rarity:*** Commonplace  
***Description:*** The primary occupants of Caelthys. Over the course of time humans have bred with almost every other race in the realm and can be found pretty much everywhere.  


### Dwarves
***Origin/Ancestry:*** Created by Solarin during the creation of Caelthys  
***Where they typically live:*** While Dwarves are commonly found in the mountainous areas like the Dulann Confederacy and in the Shadowscape, they have spread in fewer numbers across almost every other part of the realm  
***Rarity:*** Commonplace  
***Description:*** Dwarves are hearty and diligent with an affinity for stonework and metallurgy given their time spent in the mountains.  

### Gnomes
***Origin/Ancestry:*** Created by Solarin during the creation of Caelthys
***Where they typically live:*** Found throughout the realm, often integrated into human and Dwarven societies or in their own industrious townships
***Rarity:*** Commonplace  
***Description:*** Gnomes share the spark of creation granted by Solarin, expressing it through keen invention and vibrant curiosity

### Goliaths
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Created by Solarin during the creation of Caelthys   
**_Where they typically live:_** Mostly found in isolated, secretive enclaves far from major civilizations   
**_Rarity:_** Rare  
**_Description:_** Once a prominent race, Goliaths have largely been cross-bred into the general human population over millennia. The few remaining pure-bred Goliaths are staunch traditionalists who keep to themselves to preserve their fading lineage  

### Halflings
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Created by Solarin during the creation of Caelthys 
 **_Where they typically live:_** Spread throughout the overworld, favoring pastoral lands and bustling trade hubs alike   
 **_Rarity:_** Commonplace   
 **_Description:_** Known for their resilience and community-driven nature, Halflings are a staple of  society  

### Orcs
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Created by Solarin during the creation of Caelthys   
**_Where they typically live:_** Integrated into large cities or living in rural, self-sufficient enclaves   
**_Rarity:_** Commonplace   
**_Description:_** Despite being creations of Solarin, Orcs face social discrimination in rural areas, leading them to form secluded communities. However, in major urban centers, they have integrated seamlessly and are often pillars of the local workforce and defense

### Dragonborn
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Created by Solarin during the creation of Caelthys 
**_Where they typically live:_** Often found in high-altitude citadels, coastal dragon-ports, or large cities  
**_Rarity:_** Uncommon  
**_Description:_** Carrying the majestic breath of Solarin’s more primal powers, Dragonborn are a proud race defined by honor and duty. While they share the "Commonplace" world with humans, their low birth rates keep their numbers modest compared to other Overworld races

# Fey
### Elves
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Created by Lunira within the Feylands   
**_Where they typically live:_** Forested regions near Luniran Gates or integrated into cosmopolitan overworld cities  
**_Rarity:_** Uncommon   
**_Description:_** Originally intended as caretakers and storytellers of Lunira’s dream-realms, many Elves transitioned to Caelthys. They retain a graceful, inquisitive nature and a lingering connection to the "dream-logic" of their origins


### Animalfolk
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Created by Lunira within the Feylands   
**_Where they typically live:_** Untouched wilds, sacred groves, or vibrant festival-towns. Some have also assimilated into large overworld cities.   
**_Rarity:_** Uncommon  
**_Description:_** Since embarking out of the Feylands, Animalfolk  have brought their chaotic magical essence to the overworld, often living as nomadic performers or guardians of nature, while others have integrated into cities
***Examples:*** Satyrs, Harrengon, Aarakocra, Centaurs, Kenku, Tabaxi, Tortle, Loxodons, Minotaurs

### Genasi
***Origin/Ancestry***: Created by Lunira in the Feylands   
***Where they typically live:*** Primarily live within the Feylands or in "Seams"  
***Rarity***: Rare.
***Description***: Genasi are living manifestations of the elements (Air, Earth, Fire, Water). They are often seen as the "architects" of the Feylands' stranger terrains. In the Overworld, they are usually found near volcanoes, deep reefs, or eternal storms where their specific element is strongest.

# Shadow-Touched
### Drow (Dark Elves)
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Descended from the elves as they lived in the Shadowscape over the generations.  
**_Where they typically live:_** Subterranean reaches of the Shadowscape  
**_Rarity:_** Rare in the overworld, more common in the Shadowscape  
**_Description:_** While originally born of Lunira’s whimsy, the Drow are often associated with the more somber or dangerous aspects of the Shadowscape. Drow have evolved in the darkness and now reside in the shadows where they have an innate power drawn from the darkness. They are noticeably weaker when exposed to the sunlight for extended periods of time.  

### Duergar
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Descended from Dwarves as they lived and evolved to suit the Shadowscape   
**_Where they typically live:_** Deep caverns and cities of the Shadowscape   
**_Rarity:_** Common in the Shadowscape, rare in the overworld   
**_Description:_**  Much like the Drow, Duergar have been warped by the Shadowscape. They are stoic and grim, having traded the warmth of the forge for the cold power of the deep earth. They possess psionic-like abilities to enlarge or vanish in the dark but suffer under the harsh glare of the sun.

### Deep Gnome
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Gnomes who migrated to the Shadowscape to study its unique minerals and ended up evolving alongside its darkness.   
**_Where they typically live:_**  Cities and caverns in the Shadowscape  
**_Rarity:_** Common in the Shadowscape, rare in the Overworld   
**_Description:_** While their Overworld cousins are vibrant and loud, Deep Gnomes are muted and cautious. They have skin like smoothed stone and eyes that see through the thickest gloom. They are the premier survivalists of the Shadow-Touched races.

# Celestial

### Aasimar
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Aasimar are beings that carry a spark of the Celestial plane in them, typically by being a descendant of a celestial being. However, legends say that Aasimar can be born to regular parents if they are born during a celestial convergence and that people can attain Aasimar-like traits through direct encounters with powerful Celestial beings.
**_Where they typically live:_** Aasimar can be found anywhere but are very uncommon   
**_Rarity:_** Extremely rare   
**_Description:_**  Aasimar are not a "breeding race" in the traditional sense, but a celestial phenomenon. They are often viewed as omens—either of great protection or of impending judgment. They possess a natural radiance that can manifest as wings or halos of light.


### Tieflings
Descendants of the remnants of the Twilight Rebellion. After the end of the twilight rebellion, revolutionaries against Solarin were left marked with horns and colored skin. They primarily reside in the underdark but some have spread out to the overworld in the years since the conclusion of the rebellion.
**_Origin/Ancestry:_** Following the Twilight Rebellion Solarin marked his enemies with horns and colorful skin.    
**_Where they typically live:_** They originated in the Shadowscape but are also scattered across the Overworld   
**_Rarity:_** Uncommon   
**_Description:_**  Bearing the physical manifestations of the Rebellion—horns, tails, and vibrant skin—Tieflings are living reminders of Caelthys's fractured past. While some embrace the revolutionary spirit of their ancestors, others work tirelessly to prove their loyalty to the current world order.
